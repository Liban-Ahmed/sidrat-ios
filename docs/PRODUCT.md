# Sidrat: Product Vision & Goals

## Vision
Sidrat is the definitive Islamic education app for young Muslims (ages 5-7), addressing critical gaps in the market through an innovative "5-minute daily lessons + 15-minute weekly family activities" model.

## Target Audience
- **Primary users**: Children ages 5-7
- **Secondary users**: Parents managing learning and family activities
- **Cognitive stage**: Late preoperational to early concrete operational (Piaget)

## Core Features
1. **Daily 5-minute lessons**: Bite-sized, engaging lessons following Hook → Teach → Practice → Reward structure
2. **Weekly family activities**: 15-minute guided activities with parent scripts
3. **Multi-child profiles**: Up to 4 children per parent account
4. **Progress tracking**: Streaks, achievements, XP system
5. **Offline-first**: Core content works without internet

## Unique Value Proposition
- Curated daily lessons (vs overwhelming content libraries)
- Weekly family activities with parent scripts (vs passive consumption)
- Laser focus on 5-7 cognitive development (vs wide age ranges)
- Scholar-reviewed content with citations (vs questionable authenticity)

## Success Metrics
- Daily active usage (streak completion rate)
- Family activity completion rate
- Parent NPS score
- Content authenticity trust score

## Constraints
- **COPPA compliance**: No PII collection from children
- **Kids Category**: No third-party analytics, parental gates required
- **Offline-first**: Must work without internet
- **Islamic authenticity**: All content must be scholar-reviewed
